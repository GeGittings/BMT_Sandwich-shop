Insights

We conducted two types of interviews. 2 personal interviews and 1 mass questionnaire interview. After we had interviewed 8 random people, we found out that 62.5 percent do consider rewards programs, 25 percent were unsure and 12.5 said they were influenced by a rewards program. We also found references online that provided data to prove the worth of a solid rewards program.

Solution

From the data, above, we concluded that a rewards program that offers a simple rewarding system was the way to go.
